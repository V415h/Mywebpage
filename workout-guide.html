Workout Guide

Proper form and consistency are key! Here are recommended exercises and tips for each muscle group.

Chest

  • Bench Press – 3-5 sets, 6-12 reps. Keep your feet flat, back arched, and lower the bar to mid-chest.
  • Push Ups – 3-4 sets, 10-20 reps. Keep your body straight, elbows at 45° angle.
  • Dumbbell Flyes – 3 sets, 10-15 reps. Slight bend in elbows, stretch wide, squeeze at top.

Back

  • Pull Ups – 3-5 sets, as many reps as possible. Full range of motion, control the descent.
  • Deadlift – 3-5 sets, 5-8 reps. Flat back, bar close to shins, drive through heels.
  • Seated Row – 3-4 sets, 8-12 reps. Squeeze shoulder blades, don’t rock your torso.

Legs

  • Squats – 4-5 sets, 6-12 reps. Keep chest up, knees out, squat to parallel or below.
  • Lunges – 3-4 sets, 10-15 reps per leg. Step long, keep knee behind toes.
  • Leg Press – 3-4 sets, 10-15 reps. Don’t lock knees, control the weight.

Shoulders

  • Overhead Press – 3-4 sets, 8-12 reps. Keep core tight, press straight up.
  • Lateral Raises – 3-4 sets, 12-15 reps. Lift to shoulder height, slight bend in elbows.
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets, 12-15 reps. Pull to upper chest/face, squeeze rear delts.

Arms

  • Bicep Curls – 3-4 sets, 10-15 reps. Keep elbows still, full range of motion.
  • Tricep Extensions – 3-4 sets, 10-15 reps. Don’t flare elbows, control the weight.
  • Hammer Curls – 3 sets, 10-15 reps. Neutral grip, squeeze at top.

Core

  • Plank – 3-4 sets, 30-60 seconds. Keep body straight, don’t let hips sag.
  • Russian Twists – 3 sets, 15-20 reps. Twist fully, keep core tight.
  • Leg Raises – 3 sets, 10-15 reps. Don’t swing legs, control the movement.

Cardio & Swimming

  • Running – 20-40 min. Start slow, increase pace gradually.
  • Cycling – 30-60 min. Keep cadence steady, adjust resistance as needed.
  • Swimming – 20-40 min. Alternate strokes, focus on breathing and form.
