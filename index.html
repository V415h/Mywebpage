About Us

Mokku Squad is a fun, passionate cricket team based in Zeist and Hilversum. Formed in 2010, we are part of the Sri Lankan community in the Netherlands, playing the sport we love and building friendships.

We’re not just about cricket—we work together on green projects every summer to keep the Earth clean and spread environmental awareness.
